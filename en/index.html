About Us


Our school is specialized in English language. Pupils start to study English from the first form . We have a lot of good teachers.

Also, our school take part in different competitions and olympiads. Our pupils win prizes on these events. After your graduate from our school,
you could know English language, if you study well=).

Teachers from school №107 are very proud that they work here, and they try to instill love of English to pupils.
